## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

### The Client
**Al Hamra Real Estate Company K.S.C.C.** — one of Kuwait's leading national developers specializing in the management and operation of landmark real estate assets. The company delivers an integrated model for developing and operating mixed-use properties that elevate institutional work environments.

### The Property
**Al Hamra Business Tower** — Kuwait's tallest sculpted skyscraper, standing at 413 meters (80 floors). Designed by Skidmore, Owings & Merrill (SOM), the tower is the world's tallest curved concrete skyscraper and a landmark of parametric architectural design. The building geometry is generated by a spiraling slice subtracted from a simple prismatic volume, creating hyperbolic paraboloid reinforced-concrete walls that extend the full height of the tower. The south-facing core is clad in Jura limestone for solar protection, while three glass façades open to panoramic views of Kuwait City and the Arabian Gulf.

### The Complex (3 Components)
1. **Business Tower** — 80 floors, 62 office levels, floorplates up to 2,300 m², 43 elevators, executive floors on 74th & 75th, two sky lobbies (30th & 55th), restaurant and observation deck at the top in a 40-meter-high space
2. **Shopping Center** — 5 levels of international and local brands, restaurants, cafés, entertainment, and public services
3. **Multi-Level Parking Complex** — 11 fully air-conditioned floors, smart access systems, directly connected to both tower and mall

### The Goal
Design a world-class, immersive digital experience that communicates the tower's architectural prestige, positions it as Kuwait's premier business address, and drives leasing inquiries from government ministries, independent authorities, and major corporations.

### Target Audience
- Government ministries and independent authorities seeking prestigious national headquarters
- Regional and multinational corporations
- Institutional investors and high-net-worth decision-makers
- Architectural and real estate professionals

## 2. DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

### Core Concept: هيبة المكان — "A Place of Gravity"
The website should embody three Arabic design principles:
- **رصانة (Rasana)** — Architectural presence and weight
- **ثبات (Thabat)** — Authority and stability
- **وقار (Waqar)** — Dignity and reverence

### Design Principles
- **Restraint is luxury.** Whitespace, silence, and scale communicate prestige better than decoration.
- **Typography-first.** Oversized serif headlines, fine-weight sans-serif body, generous tracking.
- **Vertical storytelling.** Every scroll is a journey — from street level to the sky.
- **Material language.** The design should feel like the building itself: stone textures, polished concrete, glass reflections, brushed metal.
- **Light palette.** The site should feel bright, open, and airy — like being inside the glass-walled tower looking out at the Gulf.

### What This Site Is NOT
- Not a generic corporate site with stock imagery
- Not a dark/moody tech aesthetic
- Not decorative or ornamental — no patterns, filigree, or gratuitous effects
- Not a real estate listing site with price grids

- Section eyebrows: always uppercase, always DM Sans, always preceded by a short red accent line (40px wide, 1px tall)

## 5. INTERACTION & ANIMATION SPECIFICATION

### Scroll Animations (IntersectionObserver-based)
- **Fade-up reveal**: opacity 0→1, translateY(30px→0), 0.8s ease, staggered 0.1s per item
- **Slide-in left**: translateX(-40px→0) for text columns
- **Slide-in right**: translateX(40px→0) for image columns
- **Scale-in**: scale(0.96→1) for image containers
- Trigger: 12% intersection threshold, fire once

### Hero Animations
- Video Ken Burns: subtle scale(1→1.08) over 8 seconds
- Content entrance: staggered from top — eyebrow (0.3s) → headline line 1 (0.5s) → line 2 (0.7s) → body (1.0s) → stats (1.2s) → CTA (1.4s)
- Scroll hint: opacity pulse animation, 2s infinite

### Counter Animations
- Stats count from 0 to target value over 2–2.5s
- Easing: cubic-bezier out (1 - pow(1-t, 3))
- Trigger: when stat card enters viewport

### Navigation
- Scroll threshold: 60px
- Transition: padding, background, border, backdrop-filter — all 0.5s cubic-bezier(0.16,1,0.3,1)
- Dropdown: fade + translateY(-8px→0), 0.2s ease
- Close delay: 180ms (prevents accidental close when moving to dropdown)

### Hover States
- Feature cards: background color 0.4s transition
- Image cards: image scale 0.6s + overlay opacity 0.3s
- Nav items: color 0.3s
- CTA buttons: background 0.3s
- Footer links: color 0.3s

### Page Transitions
- Fade-in on route change: opacity 0→1, 0.4s
